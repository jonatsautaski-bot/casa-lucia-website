The Property

An Elegant Townhouse in the Heart of Estepona

Nestled on the picturesque, flower-lined Calle de la Cruz in the historic heart of Estepona, this brand-new 164m² townhouse is the perfect blend of traditional charm and contemporary luxury. Every detail has been thoughtfully designed with style, comfort, and quality in mind.

Step through the front door into a light-filled open-plan living space, where clean lines, modern finishes, and subtle Mediterranean design create a serene and welcoming ambiance — an exquisite lifestyle in one of the Costa del Sol's most desirable locations.

Calle de la Cruz is one of Estepona's most iconic streets — a peaceful, pedestrian-only lane accessed by arches of jasmine at either end, lined with vibrant flower pots, whitewashed facades, and traditional Andalusian charm.
